About Me

Welcome to my corner of the web! I'm Janel Lariba, a passionate third-year student pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering. With a keen interest in both software and hardware, I'm on a mission to explore the intricate fusion of technology and innovation. Through my journey, I've delved deep into the realms of digital logic design, programming languages, and system architecture, seeking to unravel the complexities of modern computing. Whether it's crafting elegant software solutions or tinkering with circuitry to bring ideas to life, I'm driven by a relentless curiosity and a desire to push the boundaries of what's possible in the ever-evolving world of technology. Join me as I share my experiences, insights, and discoveries on this exciting journey of learning and exploration.
